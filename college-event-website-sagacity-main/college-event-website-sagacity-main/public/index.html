About The Fest

What is Genesis'26?

Genesis'26 is the annual cultural and technical festival of Cambridge Institute of Technology, Ranchi (ESTD. 2001). Spanning 6 action-packed days from April 13-18, 2026, Genesis brings together students from across the nation to compete, celebrate, and showcase their talents across 28 spectacular events.

From the electrifying energy of Crescendo's Battle of the Bands to the intellectual intensity of HackGenesis's 24-hour hackathon, from the raw power of Robo War to the artistic beauty of Virasat's folk dance — Genesis'26 has something for everyone.
